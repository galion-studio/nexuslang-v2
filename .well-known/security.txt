# Vulnerability Disclosure Policy

We take security seriously at NexusLang. If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly.

## What to Report

- Authentication/authorization bypasses
- Code execution vulnerabilities
- SQL injection, XSS, CSRF
- Data leakage or exposure
- Denial of service vulnerabilities
- Privilege escalation issues

## What NOT to Report

- Social engineering attacks
- Physical attacks on infrastructure
- Issues in third-party services
- Already-known vulnerabilities

## Response Timeline

- Initial response: Within 24 hours
- Status update: Within 7 days
- Fix timeline: Depends on severity
  - Critical: 24-48 hours
  - High: 1 week
  - Medium: 2-4 weeks
  - Low: Best effort

## Responsible Disclosure

- Please allow us reasonable time to fix the issue before public disclosure
- We will acknowledge your contribution in our security advisories (if desired)
- We may offer rewards for significant findings (subject to our bug bounty policy)
