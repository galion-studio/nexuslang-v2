## Appendix: Email Metrics to Track

**Open Rate Target:** >30% (industry average: 20%)  
**Response Rate Target:** >10% (industry average: 5%)  
**Meeting Booked Rate:** >5% (industry average: 2%)

**A/B Testing Ideas:**
1. Subject lines: "Platform Evolution" vs "$300M Revenue Opportunity"
2. Email length: 150 words vs 250 words
3. Call-to-action: "Book a call" vs "Reply with availability"
4. Personalization: Generic vs specific recent achievement mention

**Follow-Up Cadence:**
- Day 0: Send initial email
- Day 7: First follow-up (if no response)
- Day 14: Second follow-up (if no response)
- Day 21: Final follow-up (if no response)
- Day 22: Move to next contact or different channel

**When to Stop:**
- After 3 follow-ups with no response
- If recipient says "not interested"
- If you get a clear "no" (respect their decision)

**When to Escalate:**
- If lower-level contact is non-responsive, reach out to higher-level executive
- If one channel (email) doesn't work, try LinkedIn or Twitter
- If all direct outreach fails, try indirect (mutual connections, conferences)

## Final Tips

**Timing:**
- Best days: Tuesday-Thursday
- Best time: 9-11 AM or 2-4 PM (recipient's timezone)
- Avoid: Mondays (inbox overload), Fridays (weekend mode), holidays

**Subject Line Formulas:**
- "[Specific Benefit]: [Concrete Number]"
  - Example: "Platform Evolution: $300M+ Revenue Potential"
- "[Action] + [Outcome] for [Company]"
  - Example: "Enable User Apps for Discord Servers"
- "Quick Question About [Topic]"
  - Example: "Quick Question About Discord's Developer Roadmap"

**Email Body Structure:**
1. **Opening:** Personalized compliment or observation (1 sentence)
2. **Hook:** The opportunity in plain language (1-2 sentences)
3. **Value:** Why this matters for Discord (3-4 bullet points)
4. **Proof:** Data, metrics, credibility (1-2 sentences)
5. **Ask:** Clear, low-commitment next step (1 sentence)
6. **Close:** Professional signature

**Response Handling:**

**If they say YES:**
- Confirm meeting time within 24 hours
- Send calendar invite with agenda
- Prepare demo, slides, and Q&A
- Follow up after meeting with action items

**If they say NO:**
- Thank them for their time
- Ask if there's someone else you should talk to
- Request permission to follow up in 6 months

**If they say MAYBE:**
- Ask what information they need to make a decision
- Offer to address specific concerns
- Propose a smaller pilot or proof-of-concept
